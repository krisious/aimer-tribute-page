Biography

Aimer (エメ, pronounced eh-meh) in france means “to love” is a Japanese recording artist signed to Sony Music Entertainment Japan and attached to the talent agency FOURseam. She played piano in elementary school and picked up the guitar in junior high school. She writes music in both Japanese and English and has a good command of English from her experiences living abroad.


At the age of 15, Aimer lost his voice due to overuse of his vocal cords, and was forced to undergo silent therapy for treatment. However, it did not end, after recovering, he got his unique hoarse voice.
