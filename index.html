Biography

Aimer (エメ, pronounced eh-meh) takes her name from a French verb meaning “to love”. She is a Japanese recording artist signed to Sony Music Entertainment Japan and represented by FOURseam.

She started piano in elementary school and later picked up guitar in junior high. Aimer writes songs in both Japanese and English, supported by her international experience.

At age 15, she temporarily lost her voice due to overuse of her vocal cords and underwent a period of silent therapy. After recovery, she returned with the husky vocal character that became her signature sound.
